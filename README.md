# 🩺 Sympto-Bot – Voice-Enabled Health Assistant

A beautiful, fully functional, single-file **voice-powered symptom checker and medical information chatbot** built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

## ✨ Features

- **🎤 Voice Input & Output** – Speak your symptoms and hear responses (Speech Recognition + Text-to-Speech)
- **📋 60+ Detailed Diseases** – Comprehensive knowledge base covering major categories:
  - Infectious Diseases
  - Chronic Conditions
  - Mental Health
  - Cardiovascular
  - Neurological
  - Gastrointestinal
  - Autoimmune
  - Reproductive, Eye, Skin, Oncology, Genetic & more
- **🔍 Intelligent Symptom Checker** – Matches your described symptoms to possible conditions with match percentages
- **💬 Natural Chat Interface** – Glassmorphic modern design, responsive for mobile & desktop
- **⚠️ Strong Medical Disclaimer** – Always reminds users to consult professionals
- **🚨 Emergency Detection** – Detects urgent keywords and advises seeking immediate help
- **📱 Fully Responsive** – Works great on phones, tablets, and desktops

## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Download** the file `sympto-bot.html`
2. **Open** it in any modern browser (Chrome/Edge recommended for best voice support)
3. **Start chatting**:
   - Type your question or
   - Click the 🎤 microphone button and speak
4. Toggle voice replies with the 🔊 button in the header

### Example Queries:
- "I have fever, cough, and fatigue"
- "Tell me about diabetes"
- "What are symptoms of migraine?"
- "Prevention for malaria"
- "Vaccination schedule"

## 🛠️ Technical Details

- **Single HTML file** – No server, no installation needed
- **Pure Vanilla JS** – No external dependencies
- **Speech APIs**:
  - Web Speech Recognition (voice input)
  - SpeechSynthesis (voice output)
- **Disease Database**: ~60 well-structured conditions with symptoms, causes, prevention, treatment, risk factors, and lifestyle advice
